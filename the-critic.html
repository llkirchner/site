About the Book

A notorious theater critic is poisoned and the prime suspect is the woman he replaced — Jessica Greer, a twenty-something Gen X freelancer whose mental health history makes her doubt her own innocence. To clear her name, Jessica must untangle the Steel City’s artistic rivalries, media conspiracies, and family secrets before the FBI catches her.

Perfect for fans of Lisa Jewell’s None of This is True and Ruth Ware’s The It Girl.
