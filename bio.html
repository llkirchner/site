For the Smart Reader Who Craves a Page-Turner

L.L. Kirchner has always believed the most powerful woman in the room isn’t the noisiest. She’s the one paying attention.

She’s spent her career paying attention to that woman, in her two memoirs, her subsequent trilogy about swimsuit models on a war bond tour taking on the mafia, and now her forthcoming series about powerful institutions versus ordinary people.

She wants to entertain you first. Let the rest sneak up on you.

I’ve been a journalist, a story coach, an award-winning screenwriter, and even an on-air host for the Home Shopping Network.

My life and work as an expat in Asia became the basis of two memoirs — and that was after I was the religion editor for an LGBTQIA+ paper, the dating columnist for an alt newsweekly, and the bridal editor for a society rag… all at the same time.

My writing has appeared in The Washington Post, Salon, and The Rumpus among numerous other outlets.
