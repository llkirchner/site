at Your Local Library

Love your library? You've always been able to request my print and audiobooks — now you can also request my e-books! Free for you, great for your library.

How to Request

  1. Visit your library's website and look for "Suggest a Title" or "Recommend a Purchase" (often under eBooks or digital services). Scroll to the list below to see if your library is here.
  2. Once you find the page, provide the book details below (title, author, ISBN).
  3. Select eBook format if asked (they'll order it through OverDrive, Bibliotheca, or similar).
